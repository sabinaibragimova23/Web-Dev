Recipe #1: Laghman

  • Time: 60 minutes
  • Serves: 4 people
Ingredients Quantity
Noodles 500 g
Beef 400 g
Onion 2
Bell pepper 2
Tomatoes 3

Step 1: Cut the beef and vegetables.

Step 2: Fry the beef.

Step 3: Add vegetables and cook.

Step 4: Boil noodles and mix with sauce.

Recipe #2: Manti

  • Time: 90 minutes
  • Serves: 5 people
Ingredients Quantity
Dough 1 kg
Beef 600 g
Onion 3
Salt to taste

Step 1: Prepare the dough.

Step 2: Mix meat with onions and salt.

Step 3: Fill the dough and shape manti.

Step 4: Steam for 40 minutes.

Recipe #3: Samsa

  • Time: 50 minutes
  • Serves: 3 people
Ingredients Quantity
Puff pastry 500 g
Lamb 400 g
Onion 2
Cumin 1 tsp

Step 1: Cut the meat and onions.

Step 2: Mix with spices.

Step 3: Fill the pastry and shape samsa.

Step 4: Bake until golden.
