Travel to Maldives

Stunning Views

The Maldives are famous for their crystal-clear turquoise waters, white sandy beaches, and amazing sunsets.

Places to Visit

  • Male – the capital city
  • Baa Atoll – a UNESCO Biosphere Reserve
  • Sun Island – for pristine beaches

Activities to Enjoy

  • Snorkeling with tropical fish
  • Diving among coral reefs
  • Relaxing in an overwater bungalow

Delicious Food

Try local dishes like Mas Huni (tuna with coconut) and Garudhiya (fish soup).
